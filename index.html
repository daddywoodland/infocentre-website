About InfoCentre?

Easily, quickly and securely share information with customers.

A single place for users to access information that you share for your products, events, special offers, anything you can think of.

What information can you share?

You can share any information in numerous different formats.

Share video, amimation and images so your users can easily & quickly familiarise themselves.

Include documents like user manuals, contact information.

Add links to common actions like navigation and purchasing.

How do users access information?

Scan the InfoCode to access the available information.

Attach an InfoCode and users can quickly access the information by opening InfoCentre and scanning the code.

Group and prioritise information so it's easy to follow, for example 'Health & Safety' information can be displayed prominantly.

Control access to your information

Target the right information for the right users.

Your information can be publicly available or restricted to specific user groups.

Users are targeted with the information they need, so it's easier to consume and understand.
